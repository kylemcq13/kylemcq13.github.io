About

Hi there, my name is Kyle, and Iâ€™m a Data Scientist. I currently work in the E-Learning industry for a company called Relias which specializes in the development of courses and training for the Healthcare industry.

Originally from Chicago, I spent ten years in Florida working as a chemist in the flavor industry where I specialized in alcoholic beverage flavor systems. While I loved developing flavors, I found out pretty quickly that I also loved working with data so I spent a lot of my spare time picking up programming languages like Python and R. In 2019, my wife and I relocated to the Raleigh, NC area and I decided to pursue a certification in Data Science while working as an analytical chemist for a local hemp processing company. After completing the Remote Data Science Certification from Thinkful, I set off on my new career path as a Data Scientist.

In my free time, I enjoy watching (and over analyzing) baseball, geeking out over homebrewing, and hanging out with my wife and our two pups.
